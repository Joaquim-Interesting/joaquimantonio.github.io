Permanently Delete Your WooShop Account


What happens if I permanently delete my WooShop account?

  • You won't be able to reactivate your account

  • Your profile, photos, videos, and everything else you've added will be permanently deleted. You won't be able to retrieve anything you've added.

  • Some information, like messages you sent to friends, may still be visible to them after you delete your account. Copies of messages you have sent are stored in your friends' inboxes.

Learn how to permanently delete your WooShop account.

Delete your WooShop account through your WooShop Settings

  1. Go to your profile.

  2. Tap on the top right icon.

  3. Tap Settings.

  4. On settings page tap Account Settings.

  5. Tap Delete Account.

  6. Continue then follow the instructions to confirm.

Can I cancel my account deletion?

It is not possible to cancel your acccount deletion, once you confirm the account deletion it is permanently deleted.
